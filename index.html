About

A little about
who I am

I'm a 1st year MA student in the Linguistics department at National Taiwan University and a member of the LOPE Lab. My work sits at the intersection of computational linguistics and natural language processing — from building diachronic lexical resources to probing how large language models handle non-standard language.

When I'm not (trying to) deep-dive into Computational Linguistics, I'm probably buffering with Gummy B on repeat.
